Activity: "Plan a Vacation"
Objective

To plan a vacation, highlighting the differences between declarative and imperative approaches.

The students in the group will be the client, and they will write down how they would talk to an imaginary travel agent.

Task

    The 'Clients' will plan a vacation but will approach it differently for each round.

Round 1: Imperative Style

How would you talk about the vacation you want to take with your travel agent, in the imperative style?

Round 2: Declarative Style

How would you talk about the vacation you want to take, but in the declarative style?

Write it out and we will discuss as a class to illustrate the difference between the two styles.
